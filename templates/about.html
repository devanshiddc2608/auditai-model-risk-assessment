Methodology

AuditAI treats model failures — not just model accuracy — as the primary object of study. A credit risk model was trained on the Give Me Some Credit dataset (150,000 applicants), then systematically audited for cases where it was highly confident (≥85%) and still wrong. This audit identifies root-cause failure patterns, quantifies their business cost, and surfaces them in real time through a live risk-aware decision tool — mirroring how model risk teams at banks and Big 4 advisory practices validate AI systems before and after deployment.

Tech Stack

Python, XGBoost, SHAP, Flask, Groq API (Llama models), Power BI, Render.
